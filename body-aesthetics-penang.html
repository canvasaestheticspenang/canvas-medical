What is Body Aesthetics & Functional Medicine at CANVAS?

Body change has two honest levers: what you build and what you reduce. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, both are pulled medically. EMSCULPT NEO combines high-intensity focused electromagnetic muscle stimulation with radiofrequency — building muscle and reducing fat in the same session — while EMBODY targets muscle toning and BTL EXION applies radiofrequency and ultrasound for non-invasive fat melting. Localised pockets can be treated further with physician-administered fat-dissolving injectables.

Underneath the contouring sits functional medicine: physician-prescribed GLP-1 therapies, peptide protocols and structured obesity management for patients whose goals are metabolic, not just aesthetic. Everything is assessment-first — body composition, history, blood work where indicated — because a physique built on a medical foundation is the one that lasts.

Medical weight management — Mounjaro, Wegovy & Rybelsus

The GLP-1 class has changed what medically-supervised weight loss can achieve — and at CANVAS it is prescribed and monitored as the medicine it is, never dispensed on request. Following assessment and, where indicated, blood work, our physicians prescribe and titrate the treatment that suits your health profile, with scheduled reviews of progress, side effects and dosing throughout.

Mounjaro (tirzepatide)

A dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist — the newest and, in trials, among the most effective weekly injectables for appetite regulation and weight reduction.

Wegovy (semaglutide)

A once-weekly GLP-1 injectable with a large clinical evidence base for sustained, meaningful weight loss under medical supervision.

Rybelsus (oral semaglutide)

Daily oral semaglutide in tablet form — the needle-free option for patients who prefer to avoid injections.

These are prescription medicines with genuine eligibility criteria and possible side effects. CANVAS treats them as a supervised medical programme — assessment first, honest suitability, and ongoing physician review — not a shortcut. They work best alongside nutrition and activity, which we build into every plan. Read more: Mounjaro, Wegovy, Rybelsus.

Muscle is the half of body contouring that dieting never builds. On genuine BTL platforms, CANVAS delivers supramaximal contractions no voluntary workout can match — thousands per session — to build muscle and, with EMSCULPT NEO, reduce fat at the same time.

The only platform combining HIFEM muscle stimulation with radiofrequency in one applicator, clinically studied to increase muscle and reduce fat simultaneously — abdomen, buttocks, arms, thighs and calves.

EMBODY muscle toning

Focused electromagnetic toning to strengthen and define targeted muscle groups — ideal for definition, core strength and post-natal or post-weight-loss restoration.

A typical course is around four sessions over two to three weeks, with results building over the following weeks as muscle develops and treated fat clears. Sessions are comfortable, clothed and require no downtime. Read more: EMSCULPT NEO and EMBODY.

Cryolipolysis — fat freezing

Cryolipolysis targets stubborn fat by cooling it to a temperature that destroys fat cells while sparing surrounding skin and tissue. The treated cells are then cleared naturally by the body over the following weeks — a non-invasive, no-downtime way to reduce localised pockets that resist diet and exercise, such as the abdomen, flanks, back and thighs.

Non-surgical fat reduction

No incisions, no anaesthesia, no recovery window — a controlled-cooling applicator does the work while you rest.

Targets stubborn pockets

Ideal for defined, pinchable areas — abdomen, flanks, back, inner and outer thighs — where fat persists despite lifestyle.

Gradual, natural clearance

Destroyed fat cells are metabolised over eight to twelve weeks, so the change appears progressively and looks natural.

Cryolipolysis is for contouring stubborn pockets, not weight loss — best suited to patients near their target weight. Your physician will assess whether fat freezing, EXION, injectable lipolysis or a combination fits the area, and set honest expectations for the number of cycles needed.

Who it's for

This programme may be suitable if you:

  • Train consistently but have stubborn fat or a muscle plateau
  • Want visible abdominal, glute or arm definition without surgery
  • Are considering Mounjaro, Wegovy or Rybelsus and want it properly prescribed and monitored
  • Need structured, medical support for weight or obesity management
  • Have localised fat pockets suited to fat freezing, melting or injectable lipolysis
  • Post-natal or post-weight-loss and want physician-guided body restoration

Energy-based contouring is not suitable with certain implants or during pregnancy, and GLP-1 and peptide therapies are prescription treatments with medical eligibility criteria — all confirmed at assessment.

Contouring vs weight management — which do you need?

They solve different problems. Contouring — EMSCULPT NEO, EMBODY, EXION, injectables — reshapes: it builds muscle and removes localised fat on a body already near its healthy weight. Weight management — GLP-1 therapy, peptides, structured obesity care — changes the number itself, metabolically and under prescription.

Many patients need a sequence, not a choice: metabolic treatment first, contouring second, once there is a shape worth refining. The assessment tells us the order — and our physicians will be straight with you about which lever your goals actually require.
