What is RF skin tightening?

Radiofrequency (RF) skin tightening uses controlled heat energy to warm the deeper layers of the skin, encouraging collagen remodelling and a firmer, smoother surface. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, RF is a physician-led, non-surgical option for skin laxity and texture.

Our RF platforms include BTL EMFACE, EXION Face, EXION microneedling RF and Jeisys DENSITY — allowing tailored treatment from gentle tightening to combined collagen induction.

BTL EMFACE EXION Face EXION Microneedling RF Jeisys DENSITY Collagen Induction Skin Firming

How it works

  1. Consultation. A CANVAS physician assesses your skin laxity and texture and selects the RF platform best suited to your goals.
  2. Controlled heating. The device delivers radiofrequency energy to warm the deeper skin to a target temperature while protecting the surface.
  3. Collagen response. The controlled heat encourages the skin's natural remodelling process over the following weeks.
  4. Series & review. RF is often delivered as a short course; your physician plans sessions and reviews progress.

Who it's for

RF skin tightening suits patients seeking gradual firming without surgery. Suitability is confirmed at consultation. You may be a good candidate if you want to:

  • Firm mild skin laxity on the face, jawline or neck
  • Improve skin texture and overall tone
  • Support collagen with a non-surgical, low-downtime treatment
  • Combine RF with microneedling for texture and scarring
  • Maintain a firmer appearance over time
