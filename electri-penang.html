What is Electri?

Electri belongs to the succinic-acid school of boosters: hyaluronic acid for hydration, succinic acid for the cellular recharge — energy metabolism and antioxidant defence — that tired skin has run down. The name signals the intent: skin switched back on.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Electri is the lighter, vitality-focused entry in our succinic line-up — suited to younger or less-damaged skin that wants radiance and freshness restored quickly, without the fuller redermalisation protocol.

HA + succinic acid Energising & radiance Skin vitality Lighter succinic option Short course Face & neck

How it works

  1. Consultation & review. Your physician places your skin within the succinic family — Electri for vitality-focused, lighter cases.
  2. Treatment session. The complex is microinjected evenly across the area.
  3. Recharge phase. Hydration lands first; energy-supported radiance builds over the following weeks.
  4. Course & maintenance. Two to three sessions, then top-ups every three to six months.

Who it's for

Electri may be suitable if you:

  • Have young-ish skin that looks tired beyond its years
  • Want radiance restored without heavy protocols
  • Run demanding schedules that show on your face
  • Are new to boosters and want a light entry
  • Prefer short courses with simple upkeep
  • May later step up within the succinic family

Electri is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Electri vs Xelarederm

Same succinic-plus-HA axis, different intensity. Xelarederm runs the full redermalisation protocol for genuinely sun-worn, tone-shifted skin; Electri delivers the energising mechanism in a lighter, vitality-first format for skin that is tired rather than weathered.

Your CANVAS physician grades your skin honestly and starts at the right rung — stepping up later is always easier than over-treating today.
