What is LinearZ?

LinearZ is a focused-ultrasound platform distinguished by linear delivery: instead of placing energy point by point, it lays it down in continuous lines. This gives smooth, even coverage and efficient treatment, which many patients find quicker and more comfortable — particularly along defined structures like the jawline and neck.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, LinearZ is favoured for jawline definition and neck tightening, where even, linear coverage produces a clean, contoured result. Like other HIFU, it lifts by stimulating your own collagen, with results building over months from a single session.

Linear HIFU delivery Even, efficient coverage Jawline & neck focus Collagen lifting Comfortable single session Non-surgical

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses jawline, neck and laxity goals and confirms LinearZ suits them.
  2. Linear treatment. Ultrasound energy is laid down in continuous lines for smooth, even coverage of the area.
  3. Collagen response. Heated depths trigger collagen contraction and renewal over the following weeks to months.
  4. Results & maintenance. Lift and definition build gradually; maintenance is planned to your response.

Who it's for

LinearZ may be suitable if you:

  • Want jawline definition and a cleaner lower-face contour
  • Have neck laxity you'd like tightened smoothly
  • Prefer even, efficient ultrasound coverage
  • Want a comfortable single-session lift
  • Are combining lifting with skin-quality treatments
  • Are building a non-surgical rejuvenation plan

LinearZ suits mild to moderate laxity; advanced sagging may need combined or surgical approaches. It is not suitable over certain conditions or during pregnancy; suitability is assessed at consultation. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

LinearZ vs Ultraformer vs Ultherapy

Three focused-ultrasound options. LinearZ uses linear delivery for fast, even coverage, strong on jawline and neck. Ultraformer MPT is the versatile multi-depth all-rounder. Ultherapy adds real-time imaging to visualise the layers.

Even linear jawline and neck work: LinearZ. Versatile depth range: Ultraformer. Imaging-guided precision: Ultherapy. Your CANVAS physician will match the platform to your face.
