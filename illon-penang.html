What is iLLON?

iLLON is a Korean skin-quality treatment in the K-beauty booster tradition — driving a cocktail of hydrating and revitalising actives into the skin more intensively than topical products can reach. The philosophy is nourishment: flooding the skin with hydration and skin-supporting ingredients for the plump, dewy, luminous quality Korean skincare is known for.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, iLLON offers an accessible route to intensive skin nourishment and glow — gentler than injectable skinboosters but more intensive than a standard facial. It suits patients wanting the K-beauty 'glass skin' look through a treatment-based approach, and pairs well with the wider skin-quality menu.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your therapist or physician confirms iLLON fits your goal — intensive hydration and glow.
  2. Preparation & delivery. The skin is prepared and the iLLON active cocktail delivered into it using gentle enhancing methods.
  3. Nourish & plump. The skin absorbs the hydrating, revitalising actives, looking plumper and more radiant.
  4. Course & rhythm. Repeated treatments build hydration and glow within a regular skin-quality routine.

Who it's for

iLLON may be suitable if you:

  • Want the plump, dewy K-beauty 'glass skin' glow
  • Have dry, dull or tired-looking skin
  • Prefer a treatment-based route to intensive hydration
  • Want something gentler than injectable skinboosters
  • Are maintaining skin quality on a regular rhythm
  • Are new to treatments and want a nourishing start

iLLON is a skin-nourishing treatment, not suitable over active infection or broken skin. For deeper or longer-lasting hydration, a physician may recommend injectable skinboosters instead or alongside. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

iLLON vs injectable skinboosters

Both chase hydrated, glowing skin. iLLON delivers actives into the skin through a gentle, treatment-based route — accessible, comfortable, with a regular rhythm. Injectable skinboosters like Restylane Skinboosters or Profhilo place hyaluronic acid within the skin by injection for deeper, longer-lasting hydration.

Gentle, treatment-based glow: iLLON. Deeper, longer-lasting hydration: injectable boosters. Your CANVAS physician will match the intensity to your skin and goals — and they combine well.
