What is Rybelsus?

Rybelsus is oral semaglutide — the same GLP-1 molecule as the injectable Wegovy, formulated as a daily tablet. It reduces appetite, slows gastric emptying and helps patients feel full on less food, delivering the GLP-1 approach to weight management without any injection. Correct timing matters: it is taken on an empty stomach with a small sip of water, then a wait before consuming anything else, so the medicine absorbs properly.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Rybelsus is offered strictly as prescription medicine within a supervised programme — the option of choice for patients who want the GLP-1 mechanism but prefer to avoid needles. Our physicians assess suitability, titrate gradually, and review throughout, alongside the nutrition and activity guidance built into every plan.

How it works

  1. Medical assessment. A physician reviews your history, medications and goals, confirming whether oral semaglutide is appropriate and safe for you.
  2. Prescription & titration start. If suitable, treatment begins low and steps up gradually over weeks to minimise side effects.
  3. Daily tablet routine. Taken each morning on an empty stomach with a little water, waiting before eating or drinking — a routine your physician will explain clearly.
  4. Scheduled reviews. Progress, tolerance and dose are monitored and adjusted to your response.

Rybelsus is a prescription medicine with eligibility criteria and possible side effects; it is not suitable for everyone, and not during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Its absorption depends on correct daily timing, which your physician will explain. It works best alongside nutrition and activity. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Rybelsus vs Wegovy vs Mounjaro

Rybelsus is oral semaglutide — a daily tablet, needle-free. Wegovy is the same molecule (semaglutide) as a once-weekly injection, with a larger weight-loss evidence base at higher injectable doses. Mounjaro (tirzepatide) is a weekly dual-receptor injection with the strongest average trial results.

Injection or tablet, single or dual receptor — the right fit depends on your preferences, goals and health profile. Your CANVAS physician will talk you through it honestly.
