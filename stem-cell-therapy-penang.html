What is Stem cell therapy?

Stem cell and regenerative therapy is an advanced field of medicine that harnesses cellular signals to support renewal and vitality. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, these protocols are strictly physician-led, with eligibility determined through consultation and medical screening.

Our regenerative menu includes mesenchymal stem cell (MSC) therapy, exosome therapy and stem cell IV infusion. Every therapy is carried out under medical supervision — we do not offer these treatments without proper assessment.

How it works

  1. Medical consultation. A CANVAS physician reviews your health, goals and history to determine whether regenerative therapy is appropriate.
  2. Screening & eligibility. Medical screening confirms suitability before any protocol is offered — safety comes first.
  3. Personalised protocol. Your physician designs a regenerative plan, whether MSC therapy, exosomes or IV infusion.
  4. Supervised delivery & review. Treatment is carried out under medical supervision, with follow-up to monitor your response.
