What is Juvelook?

Juvelook is a hybrid injectable combining poly-D,L-lactic acid (PDLLA) — a collagen biostimulator — with non-crosslinked hyaluronic acid. The HA hydrates and plumps the skin immediately; the PDLLA then stimulates collagen where it was placed, making the improvement progressive and durable rather than purely temporary.

Because it is formulated for the superficial layers, Juvelook at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, is a skin-quality tool rather than a volumiser: enlarged pores, rough texture, fine lines, under-eye crepiness and depressed acne scars are its natural territory. It is one of the most-requested treatments for skin refinement in our formulary.

How it works

  1. Consultation & skin assessment. Your physician examines texture, pores, scarring and fine lines, and confirms whether Juvelook alone or a combination protocol will serve you best.
  2. Treatment session. Juvelook is delivered evenly into the superficial skin across the area — the HA hydrates on day one while the PDLLA settles in to work.
  3. Collagen refines the skin. Over the following weeks, collagen builds at skin level — texture smooths, pores tighten, shallow scars lift.
  4. Course completion. Two to three sessions around four weeks apart complete a standard course, with the result continuing to mature afterwards.

Who it's for

Juvelook may be suitable if you:

  • Are bothered by enlarged pores or uneven, rough texture
  • Have depressed acne scarring you want gradually improved
  • Notice crepiness or fine lines under the eyes
  • Want longer-lasting results than standard HA skinboosters give
  • Prefer progressive, natural improvement over dramatic single-session change
  • Are building a long-term skin quality programme

Juvelook is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active acne flares or skin infection — these are settled first. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Juvelook vs classic HA skinboosters

A classic HA skinbooster hydrates beautifully, but the effect fades as the HA is metabolised over months. Juvelook adds a PDLLA collagen stimulus on top of the hydration — so part of the result is rebuilt skin structure that persists well beyond the HA itself.

For pure glow and hydration, a classic booster like Restylane Skinboosters or Profhilo may be the right call; for texture, pores and scars, the hybrid usually earns its place. Your physician will match the tool to your skin at consultation.
