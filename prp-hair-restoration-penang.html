What is PRP Hair Restoration?

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) uses your own blood's healing machinery. A sample is spun to concentrate platelets, which release growth factors that support tissue repair and cell function. Delivered into the scalp at follicle level, those growth factors help strengthen existing hair, prolong the follicle's active growth phase and support regrowth — a regenerative approach using nothing but your own biology.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, PRP hair restoration is prepared with the Cellenis system we use clinic-wide and performed by physicians. It suits early to moderate thinning — including androgenetic (pattern) thinning and post-partum or stress-related shedding — and works best started early, often alongside medical hair therapies your physician may recommend.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses the pattern and cause of thinning and confirms PRP is appropriate — sometimes alongside other therapies.
  2. Blood draw & preparation. A small blood sample is drawn and spun in the Cellenis system to concentrate the platelets.
  3. Scalp treatment. The platelet-rich plasma is injected into the scalp at follicle level, with comfort measures used.
  4. Course & maintenance. An initial course builds results over months; maintenance sessions hold them.

Who it's for

PRP Hair Restoration may be suitable if you:

  • Notice early to moderate hair thinning or increased shedding
  • Have pattern (androgenetic) thinning you want to slow and support
  • Have post-partum or stress-related shedding
  • Prefer a treatment using your own biology
  • Want to combine PRP with medical hair therapies
  • Are willing to complete a course and maintenance

PRP supports existing and miniaturising follicles but cannot regrow hair where follicles are entirely lost; advanced baldness needs different options. Results vary, are gradual, and require maintenance. Your physician will assess suitability and set honest expectations. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

PRP within a hair-loss plan

Hair loss has causes — genetic, hormonal, nutritional, stress-related — and the best results come from addressing the cause, not just stimulating follicles. PRP is a strong regenerative tool, but it works best inside a plan: your physician may assess for underlying factors and recommend medical therapies alongside it.
