What is Luhilo Snow?

Luhilo Snow is the brightening formulation of the Luhilo line: hyaluronic acid for hydration, carrying a complex oriented to radiance and evenness of tone. Dullness has two causes — dehydration and tone — and Snow is built for the second, for skin that looks tired or sallow even when it is not strictly dry.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Luhilo Snow answers one of our most common requests: 'brighter'. It suits skin dulled by sun, city and screens — often sequenced after pure hydration, or alongside laser tone work for a compounding effect.

Brightening complex + HA Tone & radiance K-beauty luminosity Hydration included Pairs with laser tone work Course-based protocol

How it works

  1. Consultation & tone review. Your physician distinguishes dryness-dullness from tone-dullness — and confirms Snow fits your case.
  2. Treatment session. The brightening HA complex is delivered evenly across the face, with numbing for comfort.
  3. Radiance builds. Hydration lifts first; tone and evenness improve across the course.
  4. Course & maintenance. Two to three sessions, then top-ups every three to six months.

Who it's for

Luhilo Snow may be suitable if you:

  • Look dull or sallow even with decent hydration
  • Want brightness without bleaching or aggressive peels
  • Are running laser or pigment programmes and want a boost
  • Have skin tired by sun, city and screen life
  • Chase the clear, bright K-beauty complexion
  • Prefer gentle, course-based improvement

Luhilo Snow is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Pigmentation conditions are assessed first — melasma needs its own plan. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Brightening honestly: what Snow does and doesn't

Snow brightens by hydrating and supporting an even, radiant tone — it is not a pigment-destroying treatment. Discrete pigmentation, melasma and deep sun damage are laser-and-protocol territory, where our laser programmes lead and boosters support.

The honest sequence for most dull skin: diagnose the dullness, treat pigment where pigment exists, and let Snow supply the radiance layer on top. Your CANVAS physician will order those steps correctly.
