What are Dermal fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable treatments used to restore volume, refine contours and soften the signs of ageing. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, all fillers are administered by qualified physicians who prioritise balance and natural-looking results.

Our formulary spans hyaluronic acid fillers, bio-stimulators such as Sculptra and Radiesse, and skinboosters like Profhilo — so your physician can select the right product for lips, cheeks, jawline or overall skin quality.

Lip Enhancement Cheek & Contour Bio-stimulators Skinboosters Profhilo Jawline Definition

How it works

  1. Consultation & facial assessment. A CANVAS physician studies your facial proportions and discusses your goals before recommending any product.
  2. Product selection. The filler, bio-stimulator or skinbooster is chosen for the area and effect — volume, contour or skin quality.
  3. Precise placement. The physician injects small, measured amounts using fine techniques for comfort and accuracy.
  4. Review & refinement. We review your result and plan any maintenance, following a less-is-more approach.

Benefits

Natural volume

Restores subtle fullness to cheeks, lips and contours without looking overdone.

Balanced contours

Refines the jawline and facial proportions for a harmonious result.

Who it's for

Dermal fillers suit a range of goals, always assessed individually at consultation. You may be a good candidate if you wish to:

  • Restore volume lost to ageing in the cheeks or temples
  • Enhance lips subtly and naturally
  • Define the jawline or chin for better balance
  • Improve overall skin hydration and glow with skinboosters
  • Refresh your appearance with a measured, natural result

FAQ

Dermal fillers, answered.

Natural is our standard. Our physicians follow a less-is-more philosophy, placing small, measured amounts to enhance your features rather than change them. The goal is a refreshed, balanced look.
It depends on the product and area. Hyaluronic acid fillers commonly last several months to over a year, while bio-stimulators work gradually over a longer period. Your physician will explain what to expect.
Most modern fillers contain anaesthetic, and numbing cream is applied for comfort. Most patients tolerate the treatment well and describe only brief pressure.
Downtime is usually minimal. You may notice minor swelling or bruising for 24–48 hours. We recommend avoiding blood thinners and alcohol for 24 hours beforehand.
