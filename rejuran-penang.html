What is Rejuran?

Rejuran is an injectable of purified polynucleotides (PN) derived from salmon DNA — biocompatible fragments that signal the skin to repair itself. Rather than filling or volumising, Rejuran works biologically: stimulating fibroblasts, supporting collagen synthesis, improving microcirculation and calming low-grade inflammation.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Rejuran anchors our regenerative skin programmes. The classic Healer treats overall skin health and elasticity; Rejuran S is a denser formulation for depressed acne scars; Rejuran I is a gentler preparation for the delicate under-eye area. Your physician selects the variant — or combination — your skin actually needs.

How it works

  1. Consultation & variant selection. Your physician assesses skin damage, scarring and the eye area, and selects Healer, S, I — or a combination — accordingly.
  2. Treatment session. Polynucleotides are delivered evenly into the superficial skin by microdroplet technique. Numbing cream keeps the session comfortable.
  3. Biological repair — weeks 1 to 4. PN signalling stimulates fibroblasts, collagen synthesis and microcirculation; texture and resilience improve progressively.
  4. Course completion. Three to four sessions, two to four weeks apart, complete a standard course. Maintenance is typically every six to twelve months.

Who it's for

Rejuran may be suitable if you:

  • Have dull, tired, inflamed or sun-damaged skin
  • Notice loss of elasticity, fine lines or thinning skin
  • Want acne scars improved biologically over a course
  • Have under-eye crepiness unsuited to filler
  • Prefer regenerative treatment over volumising
  • Are building a long-term skin health programme

Rejuran is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, with fish allergy (discuss with your physician), or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Rejuran Healer vs S vs I

Rejuran Healer is the classic full-face treatment for overall repair, elasticity and glow. Rejuran S is a thicker formulation placed under depressed acne scars to rebuild support. Rejuran I is a lighter preparation designed for the thin, delicate skin around the eyes.

They are frequently combined in one programme — Healer across the face, S under scars, I around the eyes. Your CANVAS physician will map the right combination at consultation.
