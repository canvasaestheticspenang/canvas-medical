What is Restylane Skinboosters?

Restylane Skinboosters are the treatment that defined the skinbooster category: stabilised, long-lasting hyaluronic acid delivered as hundreds of precise microdroplets just under the skin. Unlike the HA in creams — which sits on the surface — or filler HA — which builds shape — skinbooster HA integrates into the skin itself, hydrating it from within for months.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Restylane Skinboosters are the dependable workhorse for skin quality: face, lips, neck, décolletage and the backs of the hands. The stabilised formulation (NASHA technology) resists breakdown, which is why results are measured in months rather than weeks.

How it works

  1. Consultation & version choice. Your physician assesses skin thickness and hydration needs, selecting Vital or Vital Light per area.
  2. Starter course. Two to three sessions, two to four weeks apart, place stabilised HA microdroplets evenly across the treatment area.
  3. Hydration integrates. The stabilised HA draws and holds water within the skin — luminosity, elasticity and smoothness build across the course.
  4. Maintenance. A single top-up roughly every six months typically maintains the result.

Who it's for

Restylane Skinboosters may be suitable if you:

  • Have dry, dull skin that no topical routine rescues
  • Notice crepey texture on the neck, décolletage or hands
  • Want smoother, more luminous skin without shape change
  • Prefer an extensively proven, predictable treatment
  • Are preparing skin ahead of an event season
  • Want a maintainable long-term skin quality routine

Restylane Skinboosters are not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Skinboosters vs Profhilo

Both are HA-based skin quality treatments, differently engineered. Profhilo uses ultra-high-concentration unstabilised HA injected at ten points, spreading to remodel diffusely. Restylane Skinboosters place stabilised HA exactly where hydration is needed — hundreds of microdroplets, precisely distributed, engineered to persist.

Broadly: Profhilo for diffuse remodelling and laxity, Skinboosters for targeted, controlled, lasting hydration — and the neck, décolletage and hands. Many CANVAS patients alternate them across the year under physician guidance.
