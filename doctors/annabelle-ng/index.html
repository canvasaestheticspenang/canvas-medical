About Dr. Annabelle Ng Ee Ming

Dr. Annabelle Ng Ee Ming is an aesthetic physician at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang. She earned her MBBS from AIMST University and practises across the clinic's physician-led aesthetic services, from injectables and skin rejuvenation to combination treatment programmes.

Dr. Ng's approach starts with careful assessment. She is deliberate about matching each treatment to the patient in front of her — skin type, anatomy, lifestyle and budget — rather than working from a standard menu, and she is straightforward about sequencing: what to do first, what can wait, and what isn't needed at all.

She practises under Malaysian Medical Council standards within CANVAS's physician-only treatment model. Patients value her clear explanations and calm, methodical treatment style — balanced, natural-looking results built over sensible timelines.
