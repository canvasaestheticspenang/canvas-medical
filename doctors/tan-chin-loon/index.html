About Dr. Tan Chin Loon

Dr. Tan Chin Loon is an aesthetic physician at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, with particular expertise in advanced injectables, skin biostimulation and anti-ageing protocols. He holds a Doctor of Medicine (MD) from UCSI University and a Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine from the American Academy of Aesthetic Medicine (AAAM, USA).

Dr. Tan is known among his patients for a precise, methodical approach — he takes time to map facial structure and skin quality before planning any treatment, and he prioritises patient comfort throughout. His work spans bio-remodelling and biostimulatory treatments that support the skin's own renewal, as well as carefully placed injectables designed to look natural in movement and at rest.

He favours treatment plans delivered gradually over a planned series rather than dramatic one-off changes, and he is candid with patients about what each option can and cannot achieve. Continuing education is central to his practice, and he keeps current with evolving evidence in aesthetic and regenerative medicine so that his recommendations remain grounded and appropriate.
