About Dr. Samuel Tong

Dr. Samuel Tong is an aesthetic physician at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, with a focus on facial aesthetics and non-surgical rejuvenation. He completed his medical training through the National University of Ireland and the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland (MBBChBAO), and is LCP-certified under the Malaysian Medical Council — the recognised standard for aesthetic practice in Malaysia.

Dr. Tong approaches every consultation by first understanding a patient's facial anatomy, concerns and goals before recommending any treatment. His practice centres on a less-is-more philosophy: subtle, balanced adjustments that work with a person's natural features rather than against them. He regularly performs injectable treatments, dermal filler placement, energy-based skin tightening and laser-based rejuvenation, always tailoring the plan to the individual.

Patients describe his manner as measured and reassuring, and he places particular emphasis on setting honest, realistic expectations at consultation. Dr. Tong believes the most successful aesthetic work is the kind that simply leaves a patient looking rested and refreshed.
