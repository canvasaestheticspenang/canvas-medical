What is Neauvia?

Neauvia, produced by European group Matex Lab, built its name on 'organic' hyaluronic acid — HA derived from a biotechnological Bacillus source, formulated for high purity. The range runs from hydration boosters to its signature hybrids, which blend HA with a fraction of calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) so a single treatment hydrates now and biostimulates after.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Neauvia gives your physician a range rather than a product: pure-HA protocols when hydration is the whole brief, hybrid formulations when skin also needs the collagen nudge of CaHA — a booster and a light biostimulator in one syringe.

Organic-source HA HA + CaHA hybrids Hydration + firming Range, not one product Face · Neck · Décolletage European manufacturing

How it works

  1. Consultation & formulation choice. Your physician selects from the Neauvia range — pure HA or hybrid — by what your skin actually needs.
  2. Treatment session. The chosen formulation is delivered evenly across the area, with numbing available.
  3. Hydrate, then build. HA hydration shows early; in hybrids, CaHA biostimulation adds firming over the following weeks.
  4. Course & maintenance. Two to three sessions typical, with maintenance per formulation and response.

Who it's for

Neauvia may be suitable if you:

  • Want hydration with a firming dividend in one treatment
  • Have mild laxity alongside dehydration
  • Prefer high-purity, European-manufactured products
  • Are treating neck or décolletage as well as face
  • Like ranges that scale with your skin over time
  • Want your physician choosing formulation, not just brand

Neauvia treatments are not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection; hybrid (CaHA-containing) formulations are not used in the lips or under-eye. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Hybrids: when a booster should also build

Pure HA boosters hydrate; biostimulators build. Neauvia's hybrids occupy the deliberate middle — enough CaHA to nudge collagen, carried in enough HA to hydrate properly. For skin that is both dry and beginning to slacken, that twin action can outperform either class alone.

When laxity dominates, a dedicated biostimulator like Radiesse serves better; when dryness dominates, a pure booster does. The CANVAS formulary holds all three answers, so the diagnosis — not the stockroom — decides.
