What is Ultraformer MPT?

Ultraformer MPT is a focused-ultrasound (HIFU) platform prized for versatility: a range of interchangeable cartridges lets the physician treat multiple depths in one session — superficial layers for fine tightening and texture, deeper layers including the foundation for lifting. Its Micro Pulsed Technology delivers the energy efficiently, making treatment faster and more comfortable than older HIFU.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Ultraformer MPT is our all-rounder for non-surgical lifting and contouring — cheeks, jawline, under-chin and neck, with body applications too. Because it can address several depths, your physician can tailor a genuinely customised lift-and-tighten plan in a single, comfortable session.

Focused ultrasound (HIFU) Multi-depth cartridges Lift · Tighten · Contour Micro Pulsed Technology Face & body Single session

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses laxity and contour goals and selects the cartridge depths to treat.
  2. Multi-depth treatment. Focused ultrasound is delivered at the chosen depths — deep for lift, superficial for tightening.
  3. Collagen response. Heated points trigger collagen contraction and renewal over the following weeks to months.
  4. Results & maintenance. Lift and tightening build gradually; maintenance is planned to your response.

Who it's for

Ultraformer MPT may be suitable if you:

  • Have mild to moderate facial or neck laxity
  • Want lifting and contouring tailored across depths
  • Prefer a comfortable, efficient single-session HIFU
  • Want jawline, under-chin or cheek definition without surgery
  • Are combining lifting with skin-quality treatments
  • Are building a non-surgical rejuvenation plan

Ultraformer MPT suits mild to moderate laxity; advanced sagging may need combined or surgical approaches. It is not suitable over certain conditions or during pregnancy; suitability is assessed at consultation. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Ultraformer vs Ultherapy vs LinearZ

Three focused-ultrasound approaches. Ultraformer MPT is the versatile all-rounder, treating multiple depths efficiently. Ultherapy adds real-time imaging to visualise the layers treated. LinearZ uses linear delivery for faster, even coverage, strong on the jawline and neck.

Versatile multi-depth lift: Ultraformer. Imaging-guided: Ultherapy. Fast even linear coverage: LinearZ. Your CANVAS physician will match the platform to your face and goals.
