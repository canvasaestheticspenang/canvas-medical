What is EMBODY?

EMBODY is a focused electromagnetic muscle-toning treatment that stimulates strong, involuntary contractions in a targeted muscle group — training it far beyond what voluntary exercise achieves in the same time. Where EMSCULPT NEO adds radiofrequency to also reduce fat, EMBODY concentrates purely on the muscle: strengthening, toning and defining.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, EMBODY suits patients whose goal is tone and strength rather than fat reduction — core definition, glute strengthening, and restoring muscle tone after pregnancy or significant weight loss. Sessions are clothed, comfortable and require no recovery time.

Focused electromagnetic toning Muscle strength & definition Core · Glutes · Arms · Thighs ~30-minute sessions No downtime Post-natal restoration

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms the muscle group and that toning — rather than fat reduction — matches your goal.
  2. Applicator placement. The applicator is secured over the target muscle; you lie comfortably and clothed.
  3. Muscle training. Focused electromagnetic energy drives strong contractions that strengthen and tone the muscle over roughly 30 minutes.
  4. Course & results. A typical course is four to six sessions over two to three weeks, with tone building over the following weeks.

Who it's for

EMBODY may be suitable if you:

  • Want muscle tone and definition rather than fat reduction
  • Are restoring core or glute strength after pregnancy or weight loss
  • Have a training plateau in a specific muscle group
  • Are near your target weight and want to firm and define
  • Prefer a no-downtime addition to your fitness routine
  • Want toning coordinated with wider body goals

EMBODY is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in the treatment area, during pregnancy, or with certain medical conditions. It tones muscle and does not reduce overall weight. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

EMBODY vs EMSCULPT NEO

Both drive muscle contractions beyond voluntary effort; the difference is fat. EMSCULPT NEO adds synchronised radiofrequency to reduce the fat layer while it builds muscle — build and burn together. EMBODY focuses purely on muscle toning and strengthening, without the fat-reduction component.

If you carry a fat layer over the muscle you want to define, NEO does both jobs; if your goal is pure tone and strength, EMBODY is the focused tool. Your CANVAS physician will match the platform to your body and goal.
