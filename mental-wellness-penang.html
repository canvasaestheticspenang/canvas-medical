What is Mental Wellness & Sleep Medicine at CANVAS?

EXOMIND is BTL's neuro-regenerative therapy built on transcranial magnetic stimulation (TMS) — a technology with more than two decades of clinical research and regulatory clearances in the treatment of mood disorders. Focused magnetic pulses pass painlessly through the scalp and stimulate the prefrontal cortex, the region governing mood, motivation and executive control, encouraging the neuroplasticity that underlies lasting change in how the brain regulates itself.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, EXOMIND anchors our mental wellness and sleep programme: a medication-free, non-invasive option for low mood, mental fatigue, stress overload and the racing mind that will not let sleep come. Sessions are seated, around half an hour, and physician-supervised from screening to review — mental wellness handled with the same clinical seriousness as everything else in the building.

How it works

  1. Screening & consultation. A physician reviews your history, screens for contraindications such as metallic head implants or seizure history, and discusses what EXOMIND can realistically address — including when referral to a psychiatrist is the more appropriate path.
  2. Comfortable seated sessions. The applicator rests over the prefrontal cortex and delivers focused magnetic pulses for around thirty minutes. Most people describe a light tapping sensation.
  3. A short course. Programmes typically run around six sessions across a few weeks — no downtime, straight back to your day after each.
  4. Review & maintenance. Mood, focus and sleep response are reviewed with your physician, and maintenance is scheduled only if it earns its place.

Who it's for

This programme may be suitable if you:

  • Feel persistently flat, unmotivated or mentally fatigued
  • Struggle with focus, mental clarity or decision fatigue
  • Carry chronic stress that rest no longer resolves
  • Lie awake with a racing mind and unrefreshing sleep
  • Want a medication-free option before, or alongside, other support
  • Are managing high-performance careers and want cognitive maintenance

EXOMIND is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in or near the head, a history of seizures, or certain neurological conditions — screened before treatment. It is a wellness therapy: significant depression, anxiety disorders or suspected sleep disorders deserve specialist medical care, and our physicians will say so and refer you when that is what you need.

Where EXOMIND fits in sleep medicine

Poor sleep is rarely just a night-time problem — it is often a daytime nervous system problem that follows you to bed. By working on the prefrontal circuitry involved in mood and rumination, EXOMIND addresses one of the common upstream drivers of poor sleep onset: the mind that will not switch off.

Within the CANVAS programme it pairs naturally with the evening contrast therapy regime — heat-dominant sauna sessions whose post-session core temperature drop is a physiological sleep cue — and with physician consultation on sleep habits and health. Where assessment suggests a clinical sleep disorder such as sleep apnoea, we refer for formal investigation rather than treat around it.
