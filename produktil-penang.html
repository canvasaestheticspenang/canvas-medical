What is Produktil?

Produktil belongs to the nutrient-revitalisation school of skinboosters: hyaluronic acid for hydration, carrying amino acids — the literal building blocks fibroblasts assemble into collagen and elastin. The premise is simple biochemistry: hydrate the workshop and stock its shelves, and the skin's own production line does the rest.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Produktil suits skin that is tired rather than damaged — stressed, under-slept, recovering from sun or seasons of neglect — where gentle resupply over a course restores quality without drama.

HA + amino acids Nutrient revitalisation Elasticity & recovery Tired-skin specialist Gentle course protocol Face & neck

How it works

  1. Consultation & review. Your physician confirms tired, under-supplied skin is the case — and Produktil the right resupply.
  2. Treatment session. The HA-amino acid complex is microinjected evenly across the area.
  3. Production resumes. Hydration plus raw materials support collagen synthesis over the following weeks.
  4. Course & maintenance. Three to four sessions, then top-ups every four to six months.

Who it's for

Produktil may be suitable if you:

  • Have tired, stressed or under-recovered skin
  • Notice early elasticity loss without major damage
  • Are rebuilding after sun exposure or neglect
  • Want gentle revitalisation before stronger interventions
  • Prefer nutritive approaches to skin quality
  • Respond well to steady course-based care

Produktil is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Produktil vs Jalupro

Both pair HA with amino acids, and both feed collagen synthesis — the difference is formulation nuance and protocol fit. Jalupro brings a celebrated under-eye pedigree and an HMW version for deeper lines; Produktil runs as a straightforward full-face revitalisation course.

In practice your CANVAS physician chooses by zone and history — and the two can alternate across a year of maintenance without conflict.
