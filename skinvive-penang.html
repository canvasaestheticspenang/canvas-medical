What is SkinVIVE?

SkinVIVE is a microdroplet hyaluronic acid treatment from the Juvéderm family, designed for one specific, high-impact job: improving the smoothness and hydration of cheek skin. Fine droplets of HA integrate into the skin itself — no volume, no shape change — leaving the cheeks measurably smoother and visibly more luminous.

Its appeal at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, is efficiency: a single short session, minimal downtime, and a glow that lasts up to around six months. For patients who want an immediate, low-commitment skin quality upgrade — before a wedding season, an event, or simply because the mirror says so — SkinVIVE is the shortest path.

Cheek smoothness Lasting glow — up to 6 months Single-session treatment No volume change Juvéderm quality Minimal downtime

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms SkinVIVE fits your goal — cheek smoothness and glow — or recommends alternatives for broader needs.
  2. Single treatment session. HA microdroplets are placed evenly through the cheek skin in a short, comfortable session.
  3. Hydration integrates. Over the following days the HA settles into the skin, raising hydration and smoothness.
  4. Enjoy, then repeat as wanted. The result typically lasts up to six months; a single repeat session refreshes it.

Who it's for

SkinVIVE may be suitable if you:

  • Want smoother, glowier cheek skin without changing your face
  • Have an event or season you want your skin ready for
  • Prefer a single-session treatment over a course
  • Are new to injectables and want a gentle, low-commitment start
  • Wear makeup that settles into dry cheek texture
  • Want to complement fillers or Botox with skin quality

SkinVIVE is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. It treats cheek skin quality — broader hydration needs may suit a full skinbooster programme instead. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

SkinVIVE vs Restylane Skinboosters

Both are microdroplet HA treatments; the difference is scope and rhythm. SkinVIVE is focused — cheeks, one session, up to six months of smoothness. Restylane Skinboosters are a programme — a starter course across face, neck, décolletage or hands, then maintenance.

If your concern is cheek glow with minimum commitment, SkinVIVE wins on simplicity. If your skin needs hydration more broadly, the Skinboosters programme repays the extra sessions. Your physician will be direct about which fits.
