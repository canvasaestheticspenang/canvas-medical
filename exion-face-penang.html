What is EXION Face?

EXION Face is BTL's radiofrequency skin platform, combining monopolar RF — which heats the deeper skin to stimulate collagen and firming — with targeted ultrasound that BTL reports boosts the skin's own hyaluronic acid production. The result is a treatment that tightens and hydrates from within, improving firmness, fine lines and overall skin quality without injections.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, EXION Face suits patients who want firmer, plumper, better-hydrated skin and a smoother texture — a quality upgrade rather than a dramatic lift. It pairs naturally with EMFACE for muscle lift or HIFU for deeper structural work, and is comfortable enough to be a regular part of a skin-quality programme.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms EXION Face fits your goals — firmer, more hydrated, smoother skin.
  2. Applicator treatment. Radiofrequency heats the deeper skin while targeted ultrasound supports hydration; the session is warm and comfortable.
  3. Build phase. Over the following weeks collagen firms the skin and hyaluronic acid improves plumpness and glow.
  4. Course & results. A typical course is four sessions over several weeks, with quality building progressively.

Who it's for

EXION Face may be suitable if you:

  • Want firmer, plumper, better-hydrated skin without injections
  • Notice early laxity, fine lines or dull texture
  • Prefer a comfortable, no-downtime skin-quality treatment
  • Are combining skin quality with a muscle lift or deeper tightening
  • Want a maintainable regular treatment
  • Are building a non-surgical rejuvenation plan

EXION Face is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in the treatment area, during pregnancy, or with certain medical conditions. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

EXION Face vs EXION Microneedling RF

Same platform family, different applicator and depth. EXION Face works without needles — RF and ultrasound over the skin surface for tightening and hydration. EXION Microneedling RF uses fine needles to deliver radiofrequency deeper into the dermis for stronger remodelling of scars, pores and laxity.

Comfortable surface tightening: EXION Face. Deeper, more corrective remodelling with some downtime: microneedling RF. Your CANVAS physician will match the applicator to your skin and goal.
