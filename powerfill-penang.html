What is Powerfill?

Powerfill is a poly-D,L-lactic acid (PDLLA) biostimulator formulated for volume at scale. Like its PDLLA siblings, it does not fill directly — its microparticles seed the tissue and recruit your own collagen, rebuilding volume progressively so results read as regained fullness rather than injected product.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Powerfill covers the larger jobs: broad facial deflation, body contouring support — and it is the product behind our physician-performed male enhancement procedures, discussed candidly within our sexual health programme.

How it works

  1. Consultation & planning. Your physician maps the zone, realistic vial counts and session numbers — private consultation for enhancement procedures.
  2. Treatment session. Reconstituted Powerfill is distributed across the planned area, with numbing for comfort.
  3. Collagen volume builds. Over two to three months your own collagen develops where the PDLLA was placed.
  4. Course & review. Two to three sessions complete most plans, with results reviewed as they mature.

Who it's for

Powerfill may be suitable if you:

  • Have broad facial or body volume loss needing meaningful correction
  • Prefer collagen-built volume over implanted gel at larger scales
  • Are considering male enhancement performed by a physician
  • Want progressive, natural-looking change over sudden transformation
  • Are comfortable with a short course of sessions
  • Value durability measured in years

Powerfill is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection in the treatment area. Enhancement procedures are consultation-gated with candid discussion of realistic outcomes. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.
