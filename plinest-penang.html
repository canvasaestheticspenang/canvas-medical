What is Plinest?

Plinest is a polynucleotide (PN) injectable from Mastelli of Italy — the laboratory whose research effectively created the polynucleotide category. Highly purified PN fragments signal the skin to repair itself: stimulating fibroblasts, supporting the extracellular matrix, improving hydration and restoring elasticity to skin that has thinned or weathered.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Plinest anchors bio-restructuring programmes for ageing, dull or fragile skin — with Plinest Eye offering a dedicated formulation for dark-circle-prone, crepey under-eye skin, and the standard formulation treating face, neck and beyond.

How it works

  1. Consultation & variant selection. Your physician assesses skin condition and the eye area, selecting Plinest, Plinest Eye or a combination.
  2. Treatment session. Polynucleotides are microinjected evenly across the treatment area. Numbing cream keeps the session comfortable.
  3. Bio-restructuring — weeks 1 to 4. PN signalling stimulates fibroblasts and matrix repair; hydration, elasticity and resilience improve progressively.
  4. Course & maintenance. Three to four sessions, two to three weeks apart, complete a course; maintenance is typically every six to twelve months.

Who it's for

Plinest may be suitable if you:

  • Have thinning, fragile or weathered skin that needs genuine repair
  • Notice crepiness or dark-circle-prone under-eye skin
  • Want a regenerative foundation before other aesthetic work
  • Prefer biological repair over volumising or masking
  • Are managing early ageing on the neck as well as the face
  • Value products with deep clinical heritage

Plinest is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, with fish allergy (discuss with your physician), or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Plinest vs Rejuran

Both are polynucleotide therapies with the same core mechanism — PN fragments signalling skin repair. Rejuran is the Korean interpretation, Plinest the Italian original from Mastelli's research. Formulation nuances differ, and clinics often observe slightly different textures and protocols between them.

At CANVAS both are on formulary, along with Rejuran's scar and eye variants. Your physician will recommend the PN — or sequence — that fits your skin, rather than defaulting to one brand.
