What is Novuma?

Novuma is a biostimulatory injectable based on calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) — microspheres of a mineral compound chemically similar to bone, suspended in a gel carrier. CaHA is one of the best-established regenerative materials in aesthetics: as the microspheres integrate, they act as a scaffold on which your own collagen and elastin rebuild.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Novuma broadens our CaHA options alongside Radiesse — giving your physician flexibility in matching product character, dilution and cost to the zone being treated, from jawline support to diluted firming protocols.

CaHA microspheres Collagen & elastin Firmness & contour Established material class Physician-selected dilution Long maintenance arc

How it works

  1. Consultation & product selection. Your physician assesses the zone and chooses between the formulary's CaHA options — and their dilution — for your goal.
  2. Treatment session. Novuma is placed at the appropriate depth for structure or distributed for firming. Numbing keeps it comfortable.
  3. Biostimulation phase. Over eight to twelve weeks, CaHA stimulates collagen and elastin where it was placed.
  4. Review & maintenance. Results are reviewed and maintenance planned around your response — typically a year or more.

Who it's for

Novuma may be suitable if you:

  • Want firmer, better-supported skin on the lower face or jawline
  • Are suited to CaHA biostimulation per physician assessment
  • Prefer regenerative firming over pure volume replacement
  • Have early laxity you would like addressed before it advances
  • Value long-maintenance treatments over frequent repeats
  • Are combining biostimulation with energy-based tightening

Novuma is not used in the lips or immediately under the eyes, cannot be dissolved once placed, and is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Novuma vs Radiesse

Both are calcium hydroxylapatite biostimulators, and both work the same fundamental way: CaHA microspheres scaffold your own collagen and elastin. Differences lie in formulation character — gel feel, particle profile, handling — and in how each suits a given zone and dilution protocol.

At CANVAS both sit on the formulary, with Radiesse the long-established reference. Your physician selects between them case by case; the mechanism you are buying is the same proven CaHA biology.
