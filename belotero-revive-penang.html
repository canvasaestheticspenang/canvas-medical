What is Belotero Revive?

Belotero Revive is a skinbooster from Merz — the German house behind Belotero fillers and Radiesse — pairing hyaluronic acid with glycerol, a humectant that multiplies the water-binding effect and supports the skin barrier. The combination targets the earliest, most reversible stage of photoageing: dryness, dullness, fine lines and slackening elasticity.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang — where sun exposure is a daily reality — Belotero Revive suits patients seeing the first sun-driven changes in their skin. Clinical studies on the product report improved hydration, elasticity and skin smoothness sustained for months after a course.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms early photoageing is the target — and Belotero Revive the right booster among the formulary's options.
  2. Starter course. Three sessions around four weeks apart deliver the HA-glycerol complex evenly across the area.
  3. Hydration compounds. Water-binding and barrier support build session on session; smoothness and glow follow.
  4. Maintenance. A top-up every four to six months sustains the result.

Who it's for

Belotero Revive may be suitable if you:

  • See early sun-driven changes — dryness, dullness, fine lines
  • Spend real time in Malaysian sun and want proactive care
  • Want hydration with measurable elasticity improvement
  • Prefer products with brand-specific clinical studies
  • Are treating neck or décolletage alongside the face
  • Like a predictable course-plus-maintenance rhythm

Belotero Revive is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Belotero Revive vs Profhilo

Both are HA-based and both improve skin quality, but the design targets differ. Profhilo is a remodeller — ultra-high-concentration HA spreading from ten points to address laxity diffusely. Belotero Revive is a photoageing booster: HA plus glycerol placed across the skin to rebuild hydration, smoothness and elasticity where sun has taken them first.

Younger skin with sun-driven dullness often suits Revive; laxity-led ageing leans Profhilo. Your CANVAS physician will read your skin and say which — or in what sequence.
