What is Gouri?

Gouri is the first fully solubilised, liquid polycaprolactone (PCL) injectable. Where microsphere-based biostimulators act at the specific depots where they are placed, Gouri's liquid formulation spreads through the facial tissue from a few strategic injection points — turning the entire face into the treatment area.

The result, delivered at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, is an all-over collagen stimulus: firmer, more elastic, more luminous skin rather than change concentrated in one zone. Gouri suits patients whose complaint is not a specific hollow but a general loss of skin quality, tone and glow.

Whole-face effect Liquid PCL — no depots Collagen stimulation Firmness & elasticity Skin glow Short sessions

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms whether whole-face biostimulation fits your goals, or whether targeted work would serve you better.
  2. Treatment session. Gouri is injected at a few precise points per side; the liquid formulation then disperses through the facial tissue on its own.
  3. Collagen builds. Over the following weeks the dispersed PCL stimulates collagen across the face — firmness and glow improve progressively.
  4. Course completion. Two to three sessions around four weeks apart complete a standard programme, with maintenance planned around your response.

Who it's for

Gouri may be suitable if you:

  • Feel your whole face has lost firmness, bounce or glow
  • Want collagen stimulation without volume change
  • Prefer few injection points and short sessions
  • Are building a long-term skin quality programme
  • Want a regenerative base before or alongside other treatments
  • Have early laxity you'd like to address before it advances

Gouri is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active skin infection. It stimulates quality and firmness rather than adding volume — expectations are aligned at consultation. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Gouri vs Profhilo

Both spread through the tissue and both improve skin quality — but by different mechanisms. Profhilo is ultra-high-concentration hyaluronic acid: it hydrates profoundly and stimulates collagen and elastin via that hydration. Gouri is liquid PCL: a direct collagen stimulant with no hydration payload, generally chosen when firmness and elasticity are the primary complaint rather than dryness or dullness.

They are frequently sequenced together at CANVAS — Gouri for the collagen scaffold, Profhilo for hydration and glow. Your physician will advise the order that suits your skin.
