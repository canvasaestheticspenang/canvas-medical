What is Radiesse?

Radiesse is an injectable of calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) microspheres — a material chemically similar to the mineral component of bone — suspended in a smooth gel carrier. It delivers two effects in one: the gel provides immediate volume and lift the day you are treated, while the CaHA microspheres go on to stimulate your own collagen and elastin for months afterwards.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Radiesse is used both ways: classically, for strong, immediate definition at the jawline, chin and cheeks — and in hyperdilute protocols, spread thinly under the skin of the face, neck, hands or body purely as a skin-tightening biostimulator.

How it works

  1. Consultation & protocol choice. Your physician determines whether classic Radiesse (structure), hyperdilute Radiesse (skin tightening), or a combination serves your goals.
  2. Treatment session. Product is placed precisely where structure is needed, or distributed as a hyperdilute layer across the treatment area. Numbing keeps the session comfortable.
  3. Immediate result, then building. You leave with visible correction from the gel carrier. Over the next eight to twelve weeks, CaHA stimulates your own collagen and elastin.
  4. Review & maintenance. Results are reviewed at follow-up. Hyperdilute protocols often involve two to three sessions; classic correction is typically maintained around twelve to eighteen months.

Who it's for

Radiesse may be suitable if you:

  • Want visible jawline, chin or cheek definition without surgery
  • Have skin laxity on the neck, hands or body that creams cannot address
  • Prefer a treatment that keeps working after the appointment
  • Have deeper folds that lighter fillers have struggled to support
  • Want a biostimulator with immediate, reviewable results
  • Are planning a combination programme with energy-based treatments

Radiesse is not used in the lips or immediately under the eyes, and is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection in the treatment area. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Classic vs hyperdilute Radiesse

Classic Radiesse is placed at depth in targeted zones for structure — a defined jawline, a projected chin, restored cheeks. Hyperdilute Radiesse is the same product mixed with saline and lidocaine, spread as a fine biostimulatory layer under larger areas of skin: the neck, the backs of the hands, the arms, or crepey areas of the face and body.

The classic protocol behaves like a long-lasting structural filler with a regenerative bonus. The hyperdilute protocol behaves like a pure skin-tightening treatment. Many CANVAS patients benefit from both, in different areas, within one plan.
