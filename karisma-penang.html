What is Karisma?

Karisma is a regenerative injectable from Italy that combines hyaluronic acid with collagen technology in a single soft-gel formulation. The philosophy is integration rather than augmentation: the complex is designed to blend into the dermis, supporting the skin's own matrix — hydration from the HA, structural support from the collagen component.

The result at CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, is a supple, natural improvement in skin quality — elasticity, fine lines, dermal thickness — with a formulation soft enough for delicate territory such as the under-eye area and neck, where firmer products are unsuitable.

HA + collagen hybrid Regenerative skin quality Under-eye capable Neck & fine lines Soft tissue integration Natural, supple finish

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses skin quality, elasticity and delicate-area needs, and confirms Karisma's fit within your plan.
  2. Treatment session. The HA-collagen complex is placed evenly through the treatment area, integrating softly into the dermis.
  3. Matrix support builds. Over the following weeks, hydration and dermal support improve — skin gains suppleness and resilience.
  4. Course & maintenance. Two to three sessions around four weeks apart complete a course; maintenance is typically every six to nine months.

Who it's for

Karisma may be suitable if you:

  • Notice crepiness, fine lines or loss of suppleness
  • Want the under-eye or neck treated with something appropriately gentle
  • Prefer regenerative skin quality work over volume
  • Have skin that feels thinner or less resilient than it was
  • Are combining skin quality with other treatments in a plan
  • Value soft, natural-looking, integrated results

Karisma is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Collagen-containing products are screened for allergy suitability at consultation. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Where Karisma fits among skinboosters

Pure HA boosters hydrate; biostimulators rebuild; Karisma sits deliberately between — hydration from HA plus direct collagen-technology support for the dermal matrix, in a formulation soft enough for delicate areas. It suits skin whose complaint is quality and resilience rather than volume or severe laxity.

In CANVAS programmes it often follows or alternates with Profhilo or Jalupro, or supports the under-eye alongside Rejuran I. Your physician will place it where it earns its keep.
