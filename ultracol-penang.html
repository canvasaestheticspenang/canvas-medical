What is Ultracol?

Ultracol is an injectable of micronised polydioxanone (PDO) — the biodegradable material used in dissolvable surgical sutures and PDO thread lifts for decades. Rather than threads, Ultracol delivers PDO as fine microparticles in a smooth gel, placing collagen stimulation exactly where a delicate line or fold needs support.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Ultracol earns its place in zones where a conventional filler can look or feel heavy — the nasolabial fold, the under-eye area, fine static lines. The correction builds as your own collagen forms around the resorbing particles, so the finish stays soft and natural.

PDO microparticles Nasolabial folds Under-eye lines Collagen stimulation Soft, natural finish Thread-lift material, filler form

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician examines the fold or line and confirms whether PDO microparticle support — or a different product — suits the zone.
  2. Treatment session. Ultracol is injected precisely along the target area. Numbing keeps the session comfortable.
  3. Collagen builds. As the PDO microparticles resorb over the following weeks, your own collagen forms in their place.
  4. Review. Results are reviewed at four to eight weeks; a second session is added only where genuinely needed.

Who it's for

Ultracol may be suitable if you:

  • Have nasolabial folds you want softened without a heavy filler look
  • Notice fine under-eye lines or crepiness unsuited to HA filler
  • Prefer collagen-stimulating correction over volume replacement
  • Have had filler migration or puffiness in delicate zones before
  • Want a natural, progressive improvement
  • Are building a regenerative treatment plan

Ultracol is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection in the treatment area. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Ultracol vs HA filler for the nasolabial fold

A hyaluronic acid filler corrects a fold instantly by adding volume beneath it — effective, but in mobile or delicate zones it can read heavy or migrate over time. Ultracol takes the regenerative route: fine PDO particles support the line while recruiting your own collagen, producing a softer correction that develops over weeks.

Neither is universally better — depth of fold, skin thickness and your history decide. Both are on the CANVAS formulary alongside conventional dermal fillers, so the recommendation follows your face.
