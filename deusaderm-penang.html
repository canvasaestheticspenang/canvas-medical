What is Deusaderm?

Deusaderm is a collagen-based injectable used at CANVAS to reinforce the skin's structural matrix. Collagen is the dermis's scaffolding protein — its steady decline from our thirties is why skin thins, creases and loses snap — and collagen-class injectables address that decline directly at dermal level.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Deusaderm complements Linerase within the collagen category, giving your physician a second formulation to match to your skin's thickness, sensitivity and goals. It suits quality-focused plans where the aim is firmer, more resilient skin rather than added volume.

Collagen-class injectable Structural matrix support Firmness & resilience Face & neck Quality over volume Course-based protocol

How it works

  1. Consultation & screening. Your physician assesses dermal quality and screens for collagen-product suitability.
  2. Treatment session. Deusaderm is delivered evenly into the dermis of the treatment area, with numbing available.
  3. Matrix support builds. Structural support and fibroblast activity develop across the following weeks.
  4. Course & review. Two to four sessions complete a typical course, with maintenance set by response.

Who it's for

Deusaderm may be suitable if you:

  • Notice skin thinning, fine creasing or lost firmness
  • Want structural skin improvement rather than volume
  • Are comparing collagen-class options with your physician
  • Have quality-focused goals on the face or neck
  • Prefer progressive course-based improvement
  • Are building a layered regenerative plan

Collagen-based products involve allergy screening at consultation. Deusaderm is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Choosing within the collagen class

CANVAS carries more than one collagen injectable for the same reason it carries multiple HA boosters: formulations differ in concentration, feel and protocol, and skin differs in thickness, sensitivity and need. Linerase is the long-established European reference; Deusaderm broadens the class.

Your physician's recommendation follows examination of your skin — and if the honest answer is that a HA remodeller or biostimulator serves you better, that is what we will say.
