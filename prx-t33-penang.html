What is PRX-T33?

PRX-T33 reinvents the chemical peel. It uses TCA — the acid behind many peels — but pairs it with hydrogen peroxide and kojic acid in a formula that stimulates the deeper skin to firm and revitalise, while sparing the surface from the frosting, shedding and downtime a traditional TCA peel causes. Applied by massage rather than needle, it delivers peel-level bio-stimulation with an immediate glow and no recovery.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, PRX-T33 is a favourite for patients who want firmer, brighter, revitalised skin with zero downtime — and it can be used all year, even in our sun, which pigment-prone patients appreciate. It layers well with skinboosters and other treatments, and is gentle enough for the neck and hands.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms PRX-T33 fits your goals — firmer, brighter, revitalised skin with no downtime.
  2. Massage application. The formula is massaged into the skin by the physician; a gentle warmth or tingle is normal.
  3. Immediate glow. Skin looks brighter and firmer straight away, with no shedding or recovery.
  4. Course & results. Firmness and glow build across a course; sessions can continue year-round with sun protection.

Who it's for

PRX-T33 may be suitable if you:

  • Want firmer, brighter skin with absolutely no downtime
  • Like the idea of a peel but not the shedding or recovery
  • Have pigment-prone skin and want a sun-tolerant treatment
  • Want an immediate glow before an event
  • Are treating neck or hands alongside the face
  • Are building a no-downtime skin-quality routine

PRX-T33 is well-tolerated but not suitable over active skin infection or on broken skin, or during pregnancy. While gentler than a traditional peel, suitability is confirmed by a physician. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

PRX-T33 vs a traditional chemical peel

A traditional chemical peel works by controlled exfoliation — the skin sheds and renews, with visible peeling and downtime. PRX-T33 uses TCA too, but its formula stimulates the deeper skin to firm and revitalise while protecting the surface, so you get bio-stimulation and glow without the shedding.

Want genuine exfoliation and renewal, and can accept downtime: a peel. Want firmness and glow with none: PRX-T33. Your CANVAS physician will match the approach to your skin and schedule.
