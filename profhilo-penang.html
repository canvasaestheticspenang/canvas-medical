What is Profhilo?

Profhilo is a patented injectable containing 64mg of ultra-pure hyaluronic acid (HA) per 2ml — one of the highest HA concentrations of any injectable on the market. Unlike dermal fillers, which add volume to a specific area, Profhilo spreads Bio-aesthetically through the tissue (via 5 injection points per side, known as BAPs), deeply hydrating and triggering a sustained release of collagen and elastin.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Profhilo is physician-administered as part of a considered skin quality programme. It is one of the most natural-looking injectable treatments available — patients frequently describe their skin as looking genuinely healthy, not treated.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses your skin quality, laxity and overall facial condition. We explain what Profhilo does (and doesn't do), and whether it should be combined with other treatments for your goals.
  2. Session 1 (BAP technique). Profhilo is injected into 5 precise bio-aesthetic points on each side of the face using a slow, deliberate technique. The product then bioremodels through the tissue over the following weeks.
  3. Session 2 — 4 weeks later. The second session reinforces and builds on the first. The full cumulative effect continues to develop after the second treatment.
  4. Results at 8–12 weeks. Optimal skin quality improvement is typically visible 8–12 weeks after session one. Maintenance is recommended every 6–12 months.

Benefits

Profound skin hydration

Profhilo delivers hyaluronic acid deep into the tissue — a level of hydration no topical serum can reach.

Collagen & elastin renewal

The proprietary HA formulation stimulates all four types of collagen and elastin, supporting genuine structural skin renewal.

Improves skin laxity

Mild to moderate skin laxity — particularly on the lower face, neck and hands — responds well to Profhilo over a course of treatment.

No volume, no filler effect

Profhilo produces no visible bump or filler look. The result is skin that simply looks better — healthier and more vital.

Minimal downtime

Small injection-point bumps resolve within 24–48 hours. Most patients continue their day immediately after treatment.

Complements other treatments

Profhilo pairs exceptionally well with fillers, RF, HIFU and laser as part of a comprehensive rejuvenation programme.

Who it's for

Profhilo may be suitable if you:

  • Feel your skin has lost its glow, hydration or elasticity
  • Have mild to moderate skin laxity on the face, neck, décolletage or hands
  • Want a natural-looking improvement in skin quality without structural change
  • Are newer to injectables and want a gentle, proven first step
  • Are looking to complement existing treatments such as fillers, RF or HIFU
  • Are in your 30s–60s and want to support skin quality over the long term

Profhilo is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active skin infections in the treatment area. Suitability is confirmed at consultation.

Profhilo vs Profhilo Structura

At CANVAS we carry both Profhilo and Profhilo Structura. While original Profhilo targets skin quality and laxity, Profhilo Structura is a denser formulation designed for deeper structural support — typically used in areas such as the cheeks, chin and jawline where both bioremodelling and deeper tissue support are needed.

Your physician will assess which product — or combination — is right for your face.
