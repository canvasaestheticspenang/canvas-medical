What is EMSCULPT NEO?

EMSCULPT NEO is BTL's flagship body-contouring platform and the only device that combines two technologies in one applicator: HIFEM (high-intensity focused electromagnetic energy), which forces the muscle into thousands of supramaximal contractions a session cannot be replicated voluntarily, and synchronised radiofrequency, which heats and reduces the fat layer above. In a single 30-minute session it works on both muscle and fat at once.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, EMSCULPT NEO is run on genuine BTL equipment under physician supervision — for abdominal definition, glute lifting, and arm, thigh or calf toning. Clinical studies of the platform report meaningful increases in muscle thickness alongside reductions in subcutaneous fat over a standard course.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms the target area and that EMSCULPT NEO fits your goals and body composition.
  2. Applicator placement. The applicator is secured over the muscle group; you lie comfortably and clothed throughout.
  3. Build & burn. HIFEM drives thousands of supramaximal contractions while synchronised radiofrequency heats and reduces the overlying fat — around 30 minutes.
  4. Course & results. A typical course is four sessions over two to three weeks, with muscle and fat changes developing over the following weeks.

Who it's for

EMSCULPT NEO may be suitable if you:

  • Train but want more muscle definition than exercise delivers
  • Want stronger, more defined abs, glutes or limbs without surgery
  • Are near your target weight and want to reshape, not slim down
  • Are restoring core strength after pregnancy or weight loss
  • Have a muscle or definition plateau you can't break
  • Want a no-downtime treatment that fits a busy schedule

EMSCULPT NEO is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in the treatment area, during pregnancy, or with certain medical conditions. It reshapes rather than treats obesity — best suited to patients near their target weight. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

EMSCULPT NEO vs EMBODY vs cryolipolysis

EMSCULPT NEO builds muscle and reduces fat together via HIFEM plus radiofrequency. EMBODY focuses on muscle toning and strengthening. Cryolipolysis reduces fat only, by freezing it — no muscle effect.

For definition that combines building and burning, NEO leads; for pure toning, EMBODY; for a stubborn fat pocket with no muscle goal, fat freezing. Many plans combine them, and your CANVAS physician will sequence what your body actually needs.
