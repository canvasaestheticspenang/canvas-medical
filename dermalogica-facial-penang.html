What is Dermalogica Facial?

Dermalogica is a globally trusted professional skincare brand, and its ProSkin facials are built around customisation: rather than a fixed routine, the therapist analyses your skin and selects from the professional range to address what it needs that day — congestion, dryness, dullness, sensitivity or early ageing. It is skin-health maintenance done properly, with professional-strength products.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Dermalogica facials sit at the accessible, maintenance end of our menu — deeply cleansing, exfoliating and nourishing the skin, keeping it healthy between clinical treatments and providing that immediate clean-and-glowing feeling. They pair naturally with our physician-led treatments as ongoing upkeep.

How it works

  1. Skin analysis. Your therapist analyses your skin's condition on the day to build the right treatment.
  2. Customised facial. Deep cleansing, professional exfoliation, extractions where needed, and targeted masques from the Dermalogica range.
  3. Nourish & finish. Nourishing and protective products leave the skin clean, fresh and glowing.
  4. Regular rhythm. Monthly treatments maintain skin health and complement clinical work.

Who it's for

Dermalogica Facial may be suitable if you:

  • Want professional skin-health maintenance with no downtime
  • Have congestion, dullness or dryness to address
  • Prefer a facial customised to your skin each visit
  • Want upkeep between clinical treatments
  • Are new to treatments and want a gentle starting point
  • Value a relaxing, restorative skin session

Dermalogica facials are gentle skin-health treatments; active skin conditions or infections are assessed first, and a physician can advise when a clinical treatment would serve a concern better. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Where facials fit alongside clinical treatments

Professional facials and clinical treatments do different jobs. A Dermalogica facial maintains day-to-day skin health — clean, exfoliated, nourished, glowing — on a regular rhythm. Clinical treatments like peels, laser or injectables correct specific concerns at a deeper level.

The best skin comes from both: clinical treatments for correction, facials for maintenance. Your CANVAS team will suggest a rhythm that keeps results looking their best.
