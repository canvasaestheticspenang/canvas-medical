What is ProAge?

ProAge is an HA biorevitaliser positioned for the skin changes that arrive with later decades: thinning dermis, chronic dryness, fragility and the loss of the comfortable 'padding' younger skin takes for granted. Its protocol rebuilds the hydration environment mature fibroblasts need to function at their best.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, ProAge serves patients — often 55 and beyond — whose skin needs restoration more than decoration: less about glow for its own sake, more about comfort, resilience and a healthier baseline on which other treatments can safely build.

Mature-skin biorevitalisation Hydration & comfort Thinning, fragile skin Face · Neck · Hands Gentle course protocol Restoration before decoration

How it works

  1. Consultation & skin review. Your physician assesses dermal thinning, dryness and fragility, and sets an appropriate course.
  2. Treatment session. ProAge is microinjected gently across the area, with numbing as needed.
  3. Restoration builds. Hydration and dermal support compound across sessions; comfort returns first, resilience follows.
  4. Course & maintenance. Three to four sessions, then maintenance every four to six months.

Who it's for

ProAge may be suitable if you:

  • Have mature skin that feels dry, tight or fragile
  • Notice thinning, crepey texture on face, neck or hands
  • Want comfort and resilience more than cosmetic glow
  • Are preparing skin before other aesthetic treatments
  • Prefer gentle, age-appropriate protocols
  • Value honest framing over miracle promises

ProAge is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Treating mature skin honestly

Mature skin is not young skin with more lines — it hydrates, heals and responds differently, and protocols built for thirty-five-year-olds can disappoint at seventy. ProAge's value is calibration: doses, depths and expectations set for the dermis actually being treated.

It also plays foundation: restored, comfortable skin tolerates and rewards further work — from Profhilo to gentle RF tightening — far better than fragile skin does. Restoration first is not caution; it is strategy.
