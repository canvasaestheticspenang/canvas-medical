What is BTL EMFACE?

BTL EMFACE is a needle-free facial treatment that works on two layers at once. Synchronised radiofrequency heats the skin to stimulate collagen and elastin, while HIFES — high-intensity facial electrical stimulation — gently contracts the muscles that lift the face, strengthening and toning them. It is the first treatment to address facial skin and facial muscle in the same 20-minute session.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, EMFACE suits patients who want a natural lift and smoother skin without injections, threads or surgery. The applicators are hands-free — you simply lie back — and clinical studies of the platform report improved lift, reduced wrinkles and better muscle tone over a standard course.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms EMFACE fits your goals — natural lift and smoother skin without needles.
  2. Applicator placement. Hands-free applicators are secured to the forehead and cheeks; you lie back comfortably.
  3. Lift & smooth. Synchronised radiofrequency heats the skin for collagen while HIFES tones the lifting muscles — about 20 minutes.
  4. Course & results. A typical course is four sessions over several weeks, with lift and smoothness building over the following weeks.

Who it's for

BTL EMFACE may be suitable if you:

  • Want a natural facial lift without needles or surgery
  • Notice early sagging of the brow, cheeks or jawline
  • Prefer a no-downtime treatment that fits a lunch break
  • Are wary of injectables but want visible improvement
  • Want to tone facial muscles as well as smooth skin
  • Are building a non-surgical rejuvenation plan

EMFACE is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in the treatment area, during pregnancy, or with certain medical conditions. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

EMFACE vs EXION vs HIFU

EMFACE lifts by toning facial muscles and stimulating collagen with radiofrequency — needle-free. EXION Face uses radiofrequency and ultrasound to tighten and hydrate the skin. HIFU reaches the deeper SMAS layer with focused ultrasound for a firmer lift.

Muscle-and-skin lift with no needles: EMFACE. Skin tightening and quality: EXION. Deep structural lift: HIFU. Many plans combine them, and your CANVAS physician will sequence what your face needs.
