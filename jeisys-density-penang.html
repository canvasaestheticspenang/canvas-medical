What is Jeisys DENSITY?

Jeisys DENSITY is a monopolar radiofrequency platform from Jeisys Medical, a respected Korean device maker. Monopolar RF penetrates deeper and heats a larger volume of tissue than many other RF types, raising the deeper skin to a collagen-stimulating temperature that causes immediate collagen contraction and longer-term renewal — the mechanism behind firmer, tighter skin.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, DENSITY suits patients wanting genuine deep tightening — softening jowls, defining the jawline and firming the neck — with the comfort of integrated cooling and even, monitored energy delivery. It complements needle-free lifting (EMFACE) and skin-quality RF (EXION Face) within our radiofrequency range.

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician confirms deep RF tightening fits your laxity and goals.
  2. Applicator treatment. Monopolar RF heats the deeper skin uniformly, with integrated cooling keeping the surface comfortable.

Who it's for

Jeisys DENSITY may be suitable if you:

  • Have mild to moderate skin laxity on the lower face or neck
  • Want jawline definition and jowl softening without surgery
  • Prefer deep, even RF tightening with comfort technology
  • Want a no-downtime firming treatment
  • Are combining tightening with lift or skin-quality treatments
  • Are building a non-surgical rejuvenation plan

Jeisys DENSITY is not suitable with metallic or electronic implants in the treatment area, during pregnancy, or with certain conditions. Advanced laxity may respond better to combined or surgical approaches, which your physician will discuss. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Jeisys DENSITY vs EMFACE vs HIFU

Different tools for the tightening-and-lifting spectrum. Jeisys DENSITY delivers deep monopolar RF for even skin tightening and jawline firming. EMFACE lifts by toning facial muscles and stimulating collagen, needle-free. HIFU reaches the deeper SMAS layer with focused ultrasound for a firmer structural lift.

Skin tightening: DENSITY. Muscle-and-skin lift: EMFACE. Deep structural lift: HIFU. Your CANVAS physician will assess your laxity and often combine platforms for the best result.
