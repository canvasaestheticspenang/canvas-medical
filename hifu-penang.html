What is HIFU treatment?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a non-surgical treatment that delivers focused ultrasound energy to the deeper layers of the skin to encourage tightening and lift. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, HIFU is performed under physician supervision as a considered alternative to surgical lifting.

Our ultrasound platforms include Ultherapy, Ultraformer MPT and LinearZ, allowing your physician to target the face, jawline and neck with precision.

Ultherapy Ultraformer MPT LinearZ Non-Surgical Lift Jawline & Neck Skin Tightening

How it works

  1. Consultation & mapping. A CANVAS physician assesses your skin laxity and maps the areas that will benefit most from focused ultrasound.
  2. Depth selection. The device is set to reach specific tissue depths, delivering energy precisely where support is needed.
  3. Treatment. Ultrasound energy is delivered in lines across the target area, prompting a natural tightening response in the deeper layers.
  4. Gradual lift. Results build over the following weeks to months as the skin responds; your physician schedules any review.

Who it's for

HIFU suits patients with mild to moderate skin laxity who prefer a non-surgical approach. Suitability is confirmed at consultation. You may be a good candidate if you wish to:

  • Address early sagging along the jawline, cheeks or neck
  • Firm and tighten skin without surgery or downtime
  • Support a more defined facial contour
  • Maintain results from previous lifting treatments
  • Explore a non-invasive alternative before considering surgery

FAQ

HIFU treatment, answered.

Most patients feel brief warmth or a tingling sensation as the energy is delivered. Comfort settings are adjusted to you, and your physician will check in throughout the session.
Lifting develops gradually over the weeks and months following treatment as the skin responds. Some patients notice an initial tightening sooner; your physician will set clear expectations.
Results vary by individual and typically last many months. A maintenance session can be planned to support the lift over time.
HIFU usually involves minimal downtime. You may notice mild redness or tenderness for a short period, and most patients resume normal activities the same day.
