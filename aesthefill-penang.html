What is AestheFill?

AestheFill is a collagen biostimulator based on poly-D,L-lactic acid (PDLLA), a next-generation cousin of the PLLA used in Sculptra. Its microspheres are porous — like tiny sponges — which allows tissue to grow into them as collagen forms, and gives a degree of early visible support while the regenerative effect builds.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, AestheFill is used where patients want the natural, progressive character of a biostimulator but with somewhat earlier visible feedback than classic PLLA offers — restoring cheeks, temples and mid-face softness in a way that reads as recovery rather than treatment.

PDLLA biostimulation Porous microsphere design Earlier visible change Cheeks · Temples · Mid-face Natural progression 18–24 month maintenance

How it works

  1. Consultation & mapping. Your physician assesses volume loss and skin quality, and plans zones, dosing and session count around your anatomy.
  2. Treatment session. Reconstituted AestheFill is placed across the planned zones. The porous microspheres provide a degree of immediate support.
  3. Collagen integration. Over the following weeks, tissue grows into the porous microspheres as your own collagen builds — firmness and volume develop progressively.
  4. Course & review. Two to three sessions around four weeks apart are typical, with results reviewed as they mature.

Who it's for

AestheFill may be suitable if you:

  • Have mid-face, cheek or temple deflation you want restored naturally
  • Like the idea of a biostimulator but want some early visible change
  • Prefer gradual improvement over an instant filler transformation
  • Want a result that integrates into your own tissue
  • Are comfortable with a short course of sessions
  • Want durable results without frequent repeat visits

AestheFill is not used in the lips or tear troughs, and is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection in the treatment area. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

AestheFill vs Sculptra

Both build volume through your own collagen. Classic Sculptra (PLLA) is fully gradual — the purist's biostimulator. AestheFill's PDLLA chemistry and porous microspheres shift the curve slightly: a little more visible early support, with the same progressive regeneration behind it.

In practice the choice comes down to your timeline, your tissue and physician judgement. Both are on the CANVAS formulary, so the recommendation follows your face rather than product availability.
