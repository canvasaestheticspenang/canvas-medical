What are Medical facials?

Medical facials go beyond a spa treatment: they use clinical-grade formulations and protocols selected for your skin's specific needs. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, each facial is matched to your skin profile under physician guidance.

Our menu includes PRX-T33 bio-revitalisation, NeoStrata and MCCM peels, Dermalogica protocols and laser toning — allowing a tailored approach to brightening, clarity and renewal.

How it works

  1. Skin consultation. A CANVAS clinician analyses your skin and identifies the right clinical protocol for your concern.
  2. Preparation & cleanse. The skin is thoroughly prepared to allow the active formulations to work effectively.
  3. Treatment protocol. Clinical-grade peels, serums or resurfacing are applied in a controlled, considered sequence.
  4. Aftercare & plan. We advise on home care and recommend a maintenance schedule for ongoing skin health.

Who it's for

Medical facials suit most skin types and are tailored at consultation. You may be a good candidate if you want to:

  • Brighten dull or tired-looking skin
  • Improve texture, congestion or uneven tone
  • Maintain skin health between clinical treatments
  • Prepare your skin for an event with a clinical glow
  • Follow a physician-guided skincare plan
