What is Jalupro?

Jalupro is a biorevitaliser built on a simple, elegant idea: collagen is made of amino acids, so supply the skin with the specific amino acids it needs — glycine, L-proline, L-lysine, L-leucine — alongside hydrating hyaluronic acid, and fibroblasts are fed as well as stimulated. It revitalises skin quality rather than adding volume.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Jalupro is used for dull, tired, finely-lined skin on the face, neck, décolletage and hands — and it has a particular reputation for the under-eye area, where its Classic formulation softens dark circles and crepiness in territory too delicate for most injectables.

Amino acids + HA Skin revitalisation Under-eye & dark circles Fine lines Neck · Décolletage · Hands Classic & HMW versions

How it works

  1. Consultation & version choice. Your physician assesses skin fatigue, lines and the eye area, choosing Jalupro Classic or the higher-molecular-weight HMW accordingly.
  2. Treatment session. Microinjections deliver the amino acid and HA complex evenly across the treatment area. Numbing keeps it comfortable.
  3. Fibroblasts fed & stimulated. Over the following weeks, supplied amino acids support collagen synthesis while HA hydration lifts skin quality.
  4. Course & maintenance. Two to four sessions complete a course, with maintenance typically every four to six months.

Who it's for

Jalupro may be suitable if you:

  • Look tired even when rested — especially around the eyes
  • Have dark circles or under-eye crepiness unsuited to filler
  • Notice dull, dehydrated or finely-lined skin
  • Want the neck, décolletage or hands revitalised too
  • Prefer gentle, cumulative improvement over dramatic change
  • Are maintaining results between other treatments

Jalupro is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection. Dark circle improvement depends on their cause, which your physician will assess honestly. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Jalupro Classic vs Jalupro HMW

Jalupro Classic uses lighter HA with the amino acid cluster — ideal for the under-eye area, fine crepiness and delicate revitalisation. Jalupro HMW pairs the amino acids with higher-molecular-weight HA for deeper lines, more support and longer presence in the tissue.

Many programmes at CANVAS use both: HMW across the face or neck, Classic in the eye area. Your physician will map the right split at consultation.
