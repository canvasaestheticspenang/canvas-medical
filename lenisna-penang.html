Lenisna in Penang | CANVAS Medical Clinic

The volumising sibling of Juvelook — a PDLLA and hyaluronic acid hybrid built for deeper work. Physician-administered at Gurney Walk, George Town, Lenisna restores volume and lifting support with your own collagen while the HA component keeps the result smooth and natural from day one.

What is Lenisna?

Lenisna combines poly-D,L-lactic acid (PDLLA) microspheres with hyaluronic acid in a formulation designed for volume and structural support — the deeper counterpart to its skin-level sibling, Juvelook. The HA gives immediate, smooth presence in the tissue; the PDLLA then builds lasting volume from your own collagen.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Lenisna is chosen for patients who want biostimulator-quality naturalness in areas that need genuine support — cheeks, temples, jawline — with a smoother, more immediate feel than pure PLLA offers, and durability well beyond a conventional filler.

Volume + lifting support PDLLA + HA hybrid Cheeks · Temples · Jawline Immediate smoothness Collagen-built durability Natural movement

How it works

  1. Consultation & facial mapping. Your physician assesses deflation and support needs, and plans zones, dosing and sessions around your anatomy.
  2. Treatment session. Lenisna is placed at depth in the planned zones — the HA fraction makes the immediate result smooth and even.
  3. Collagen volume builds. Over two to three months, PDLLA stimulates your own collagen, progressively replacing the initial presence with regenerated tissue.
  4. Review & maintenance. One to three sessions complete most plans, with results typically maintained for eighteen months to two years.

Who it's for

Lenisna may be suitable if you:

  • Have cheek, temple or jawline deflation needing real support
  • Want biostimulator naturalness with a smoother immediate feel
  • Are seeking durability beyond a conventional HA filler
  • Prefer volume built from your own collagen
  • Want a plan that can pair deep support with skin-level refinement
  • Value fewer, physician-planned sessions

Lenisna is not used in the lips or tear troughs, and is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or with active infection in the treatment area. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Lenisna vs Juvelook

Same PDLLA + HA philosophy, different depth and job. Juvelook works superficially — texture, pores, fine lines, acne scars. Lenisna works at depth — volume, contour and lifting support. They are siblings, not substitutes.

Many CANVAS treatment plans use both: Lenisna to restore the architecture, Juvelook to refine the surface. Your physician will sequence them sensibly if your goals span both.
