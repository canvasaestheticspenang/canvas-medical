What is Plenhyage?

Plenhyage is an Italian polynucleotide (PN) injectable — purified DNA fragments that signal skin to repair itself. PN therapy works biologically rather than mechanically: stimulating fibroblasts, supporting the extracellular matrix, improving microcirculation and calming the low-grade inflammation that accelerates ageing.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Plenhyage extends our PN options alongside Rejuran and Plinest, with strength variants that let your physician match the product to the zone — from resilient facial skin to the fragile under-eye area. It is repair-first medicine: the foundation other aesthetics build on.

Polynucleotide (PN) repair Bio-restructuring Under-eye capable Elasticity & hydration Calms inflammation Strength variants by zone

How it works

  1. Consultation & variant selection. Your physician assesses damage and zones, selecting the Plenhyage strength that fits each.
  2. Treatment session. PN gel is microinjected evenly across the area; numbing cream keeps it comfortable.
  3. Biological repair. Fibroblast activity, matrix support and microcirculation build over the following weeks.
  4. Course & maintenance. Three to four sessions complete a course; maintenance is typically every six to twelve months.

Who it's for

Plenhyage may be suitable if you:

  • Have dull, damaged, thinning or inflammation-prone skin
  • Notice under-eye crepiness unsuited to filler
  • Want repair-focused treatment before or alongside aesthetics
  • Prefer biological regeneration over masking
  • Are managing early ageing on face and neck
  • Respond well to course-based protocols

Plenhyage is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, with fish allergy (discuss with your physician), or over active skin infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Plenhyage, Rejuran or Plinest?

All three are polynucleotide therapies — the same repair-signalling biology from three manufacturers. Rejuran brings dedicated scar and eye formulations; Plinest descends from the laboratory that pioneered PN; Plenhyage offers strength variants that titrate the therapy by zone.

Carrying all three means your physician prescribes by skin and indication rather than by brand availability — the choice is clinical, and often patients cycle between them across a long-term programme.
