What is Teosyal Baby Glow?

'Baby Glow' is the micro-dosing protocol built on Teosyal Redensity — Teoxane of Geneva's revitalising HA enriched with amino acids, minerals and vitamins. Instead of hydrating in depth, the product is placed as hundreds of superficial micro-droplets, working at the level where light meets skin: tone evens, texture refines, and the complexion takes on the dewiness the treatment is named for.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Baby Glow is the request of patients chasing 'glass skin' — luminosity that survives without makeup. It changes no feature and adds no volume; it simply makes the skin itself photograph the way filters pretend it does.

Micro-dose Teoxane HA Glass-skin luminosity Even tone & dewiness No volume change Enriched HA complex Swiss manufacturing

How it works

  1. Consultation & skin review. Your physician confirms glow — not laxity or dehydration in depth — is the brief, and Baby Glow the right tool.
  2. Micro-droplet session. Hundreds of fine superficial droplets are placed across the face; numbing cream keeps it comfortable.
  3. Luminosity builds. Tone and dewiness improve within days and compound across the course.
  4. Course & upkeep. Two to three sessions, then top-ups as the glow softens — typically every few months.

Who it's for

Teosyal Baby Glow may be suitable if you:

  • Want 'glass skin' luminosity without volume or shape change
  • Rely on makeup to fake an even, dewy tone
  • Have dull, tired-looking skin despite good skincare
  • Are preparing for a wedding, event or photo season
  • Prefer subtle, superficial-level treatments
  • Are newer to injectables and want a gentle entry

Baby Glow is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active skin infection or acne flares. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Baby Glow vs SkinVIVE

Both chase luminosity through superficial micro-dosed HA. SkinVIVE is the single-session, cheek-focused option — simplicity itself. Baby Glow treats the full face with an enriched HA complex over a short course, suiting patients who want the glow everywhere and are happy to invest sessions in it.

Cheeks-only and time-poor: SkinVIVE. Full-face glass skin: Baby Glow. Your CANVAS physician will match the protocol to your face and calendar.
