What is Sexual Health & Enhancements at CANVAS?

Sexual health is health — and it deserves the same clinical standards as everything else we do. At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, intimate wellness concerns are handled by physicians, in private consultations, with treatment plans built around validated technologies rather than promises.

For women, the programme centres on BTL's EMFEMME 360 radiofrequency intimate rejuvenation, the EMSELLA pelvic floor chair, and the O-Shot PRP therapy. For men, it spans low-intensity shockwave therapy for erectile dysfunction, the P-Shot, and physician-performed enhancement procedures including Powerfill. Every plan begins with an honest, judgment-free conversation about what is bothering you and what can realistically help.

EMFEMME 360 — women's RF EMSELLA — pelvic floor O-Shot & P-Shot (PRP) Shockwave ED therapy Powerfill male enhancement Private, judgment-free care

How it works

  1. Private consultation. A confidential, unhurried conversation with a physician about your concerns — function, sensation, confidence, incontinence or aesthetics — and your history.
  2. Assessment & plan. Your physician recommends the modality — energy-based, PRP, injectable or combined — that actually fits the problem, with realistic expectations set upfront.
  3. Treatment course. Most programmes involve a short course of comfortable sessions; EMSELLA is even performed fully clothed. Injectable procedures are single-visit with brief aftercare.
  4. Review & follow-up. Response is reviewed discreetly, and the plan adjusted or maintained as needed.

Who it's for

This programme may be suitable if you:

  • Experience stress or urge urinary incontinence, or post-natal pelvic floor weakness
  • Notice vaginal laxity, dryness or reduced sensation
  • Are managing erectile dysfunction and want non-surgical, evidence-guided options
  • Are interested in male enhancement performed by a physician, not a spa
  • Want intimate concerns handled clinically, privately and without embarrassment
  • Prefer non-invasive technologies before considering anything more

These treatments are not suitable during pregnancy, with certain implants or active infections, or where assessment suggests an underlying condition needing specialist referral — which your physician will identify and discuss openly.

EMSELLA and EMFEMME 360 — the non-invasive core

EMSELLA is a chair — you sit on it, fully clothed, while focused electromagnetic energy triggers thousands of supramaximal pelvic floor contractions per session, the equivalent of a workout no voluntary exercise can replicate. It is best known for treating stress and urge incontinence, and stronger pelvic floor function benefits intimate wellness for both women and men.

EMFEMME 360 applies controlled radiofrequency to intimate tissue in a comfortable, short session — supporting circulation, tissue quality and laxity without surgery or downtime. Many women's programmes at CANVAS combine the two, with the O-Shot added where regenerative support is wanted.
