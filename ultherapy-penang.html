What is Ultherapy?

Ultherapy is a focused-ultrasound lifting treatment — and the one with a signature feature: real-time ultrasound imaging. Focused ultrasound reaches beyond the skin to the SMAS, the deep foundation layer surgeons tighten in a facelift, heating targeted points to trigger collagen renewal and tissue contraction. The imaging lets the physician actually see the layers, placing energy where it will lift rather than working blind.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, Ultherapy suits patients wanting a genuine, non-surgical lift of the brow, jawline and neck with the reassurance of visualised, precise energy delivery. It is a single-session treatment whose results build over months as collagen regenerates.

Focused ultrasound Real-time imaging Reaches the SMAS layer Brow · Jawline · Neck Single session Collagen lift over months

How it works

  1. Consultation & assessment. Your physician assesses laxity and confirms Ultherapy fits your goals and skin.
  2. Imaging & mapping. Real-time ultrasound imaging visualises the layers so energy is planned and placed precisely.
  3. Focused ultrasound delivery. Energy is delivered to targeted depths including the SMAS, heating points to trigger collagen contraction.
  4. Lift over months. Lifting develops gradually over two to three months and can keep improving as collagen renews.

Who it's for

Ultherapy may be suitable if you:

  • Have mild to moderate laxity of the brow, jawline or neck
  • Want a genuine non-surgical lift with precise energy delivery
  • Prefer a single-session treatment with gradual, natural results
  • Are not ready for, or want to delay, surgical options
  • Value the reassurance of visualised treatment depth
  • Are building a non-surgical lifting plan

Ultherapy suits mild to moderate laxity; advanced sagging may respond better to combined or surgical approaches. It is not suitable over certain conditions or during pregnancy; your physician will assess honestly whether it fits your degree of laxity. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Ultherapy vs Ultraformer vs EMFACE

All lift, differently. Ultherapy is focused ultrasound with real-time imaging, reaching the SMAS. Ultraformer MPT is also focused ultrasound, versatile and efficient across multiple depths. EMFACE lifts by toning facial muscles with radiofrequency, needle-free.

Imaging-guided ultrasound depth: Ultherapy. Versatile ultrasound lifting: Ultraformer. Muscle-based lift: EMFACE. Your CANVAS physician will match — or combine — them to your face and laxity.
