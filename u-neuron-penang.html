What is U Neuron?

U Neuron is an exosome-based preparation used within CANVAS's regenerative skin protocols. Exosomes are the messenger vesicles of cell biology — packets of growth factors and signalling molecules that instruct tissue to repair, calm and renew — and delivering them to prepared skin lends ageing tissue the instructions it has stopped sending itself.

At CANVAS Medical Clinic in George Town, Penang, U Neuron broadens the exosome category alongside Exoshine and Cryocord, giving your physician a third signalling profile to match to your skin and programme. Like all emerging therapies here, it runs physician-supervised, with vetted provenance and honest expectations.

Exosome signalling Skin renewal & resilience Recovery support Microneedling delivery Formulary depth in exosomes Physician-supervised

How it works

  1. Consultation & selection. Your physician places U Neuron within your programme — or selects a different exosome or simpler therapy where that is more honest.
  2. Preparation. Microneedling or a scheduled energy treatment creates the delivery pathway.
  3. Application. U Neuron is applied across the prepared skin.
  4. Course & review. Two to four sessions typical, with response reviewed.

Who it's for

U Neuron may be suitable if you:

  • Want exosome-class renewal within a supervised programme
  • Already do microneedling, laser or RF and want amplified sessions
  • Notice dullness, fragility or slowed recovery
  • Prefer topical-delivery regenerative options
  • Are comparing exosome products with physician guidance
  • Value honest framing of emerging science

Exosome therapy at CANVAS is a physician-supervised aesthetic treatment, not a treatment for medical disease. Not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding, or over active infection. Suitability is always confirmed at consultation.

Three exosomes, one honest rule

CANVAS carries Exoshine, Cryocord and U Neuron because exosome preparations differ in source and signalling profile — and because in an emerging category, physician-controlled choice beats single-brand dependence.

The honest rule applies to all three: provenance verified, expectations framed as progressive skin-quality gains, and the large-trial evidence base acknowledged as still maturing. Emerging science, handled like medicine.
